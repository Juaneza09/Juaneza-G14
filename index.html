MY 2023 Sportsfest Experience

The 2023 Sportsfest that happened last October 28, 2023 was the first sportsfest I have ever attended. Even though I wasnt one of the athletes that competed, I still very much enjoyed the experience. Me and my friends were cheering for our classmates and housemates as they played their sport. During the afternoon me and my friends were entertained by the volleyball and basketball game by the parents and staff. We even made a sign in a cartolina to show our support for our teachers playing volleyball. During the bleachering competition everyone in the house of Elizabeth or my housemates screamed our cheer on top of our lungs. I honestly didnt view the bleachering competition as a contest between the sections and enjoyed it instead. The 2023 Sportsfest was truly an enjoyable and memorable day and I hope to experience it again soon.
